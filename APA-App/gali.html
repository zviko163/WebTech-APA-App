King of the Hill

King of the Hill is a multi-player tournament

The tournament is open to all players, and the winner will be crowned the King of the Hill. The tournament is held monthly, and the winner of each tournament will receive a cash prize and a crown.

National Representation

The World Championships are held every two years, and the United States is looking for players to represent the country. If you're interested in competing at the highest level, this is your chance to shine on the international stage.
